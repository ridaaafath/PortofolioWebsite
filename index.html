Mark Lee

Singer | Song Writer | Dancer

Mark Lee is a Canadian rapper, singer, and songwriter known as a member of the K-Pop group NCT, under SM Entertainment. Born on August 2, 1999, in Toronto, Canada, Mark plays a crucial role in several NCT units, including NCT 127, NCT DREAM, and NCT U, as well as the project group SuperM. Recognized for his powerful rap skills, sharp lyrics, and charismatic stage presence, he has contributed to numerous songs within his groups. As one of the most talented idols of his generation, Mark continues to evolve in music and creativity.
